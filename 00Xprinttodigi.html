Print to Digital

As our New Jersey sites discontinued print production, we worked to push readers to embrace the digital newspaper with the overall theme that the newspaper content wasn't changing, just the method in which it was being delivered. I worked closely with Editors and our Marketing clients to deliver designs that felt fresh, consistent and engaging.

Full page ads.

Using archival images of early print editions of Newark, Trenton and South Jersey, I put together a set of ads to alert customers to the impending move to fully digital reproduction. I also researched the covers independently, using the National Archives.
